Privacy Policy

At NewsHub, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using NewsHub, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and hashed password provided during registration.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with NewsHub, such as viewed articles or search queries, to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Basic technical details (e.g., browser type, IP address) for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Manage your account and authenticate your login.
  • Personalize your news feed based on your interests (optional).
  • Send newsletters or updates if you opt in during registration.
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform’s functionality.

3. Data Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption.
  • Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
  • We use HTTPS to encrypt data transmitted between your device and our servers.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session.
  • Track anonymous analytics to improve user experience.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising. We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (e.g., anonymous usage statistics).
  • Payment processing (if applicable, with secure providers).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal data.
  • Update or correct your account information.
  • Delete your account by contacting support.
  • Opt out of newsletters at any time via the unsubscribe link.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@newshub.com
  • Address: NewsHub, 123 Media Lane, News City, NC 12345
